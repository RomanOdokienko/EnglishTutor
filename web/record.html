Record a session

Records your mic and the call's system audio as two separate channels, then transcribes with speaker separation.

When the screen picker appears, choose Entire screen and tick Share system audio — that captures your partner's voice from the call. Your microphone becomes channel 1 (you); the call audio becomes channel 2 (partner).

Channel 1 = you, channel 2 = partner. Works best with a 2-channel file.
